HOW DO YOU PREVENT AND STOP CYBERBULLYING?

A. Report it to a trustworthy adult that can assist you

- If you're at school tell the teacher

- If you're at home tell your parents

B. For serious cases, report to the police

- For very severe cases, call the police: 110 / 112

- or send an SMS to the police: 1717

C. For ongoing cases, report to the police, under the violation of the following laws

-Pasal 27 Undang-Undang (UU) Nomor 11 Tahun 2008 tentang Informasi Elektronik (ITE)

- Pasal 28 Undang-Undang (UU) Nomor 11 Tahun 2008 tentang Informasi Elektronik (ITE)

- Pasal 29 Undang-Undang (UU) Nomor 11 Tahun 2008 tentang Informasi Elektronik (ITE)
