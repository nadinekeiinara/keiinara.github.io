WHAT IS CYBERBULLYING TO YOU ?

Cyberbullying can be defined in a lot of ways, its dictionary definition being the act of harassing someone online by sending or posting mean messages, usually anonymously. However, what one thinks as cyberbullying can be different from another, each depending on their experiences of cyberbullying.

WHY SHOULD WE STOP CYBERBULLYING IN INDONESIA?

Almost 50% of teens in Indonesia have experienced cyberbullying. 2/5 of child suicides happen because of cyberbullying. Teens of indonesia, we need to come together to stop cyberbullying from causing more harm than it already has, and we need to do so now.

HOW CAN YOU HELP TO STOP CYBERBULLYING IN INDONESIA?

Always remember that no matter who you are, you can make a difference. If you see someone spreading rumours about your classmate through instagram, tell your teacher. If you see your sister getting hate messages from an anonymous number, tell your parents. If you see leaked inappropriate pictures of your friend on instagram, call the police. Act and act now.
